Silent Blade

When Ushimaru Yuki, a samurai spying on the Americans, goes missing, Ryujin Yuki has no choice but to pose as a ronin who deserted Japan. Will the townsfolk accept Ryujin? will Ryujin ever find Ushimaru? "Silent Blade" will release around the summer of 2024.
